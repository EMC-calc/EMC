Trigonometric Ratios

Definitions

Definitions for an acute angle in a right triangle:

  • Sine (sin): Ratio of the opposite side to the hypotenuse.
  • Cosine (cos): Ratio of the adjacent side to the hypotenuse.
  • Tangent (tan): Ratio of the opposite side to the adjacent side.

Relationships:

  • sin = opposite / hypotenuse
  • cos = adjacent / hypotenuse
  • tan = opposite / adjacent
  • tan = sin / cos

Reciprocal Ratios:

  • Cotangent (cot): cot = 1 / tan
  • Secant (sec): sec = 1 / cos
  • Cosecant (cosec): cosec = 1 / sin

Trigonometric Identities

Basic Identities:

  • sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
  • 1 + tan²θ = sec²θ
  • 1 + cot²θ = cosec²θ
